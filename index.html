SYSTEM
Welcome to jcdchat: a simple anonymous chat room.

No accounts. No message history. No censorship.

Messages are only seen by people who are currently connected.
If you leave, your chat log is gone.

Desktop:
- Start typing out a message.
- Press Enter to send.

Mobile:
- Tap the bottom of the screen to begin a message.
- Type your message.
- Tap Go to send.

Commands:
- /name NewName - Change your display name.
- /clear - Clear your local chat log.
